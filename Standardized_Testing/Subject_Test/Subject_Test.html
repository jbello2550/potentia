How to Register

The registration process is the same for all SAT Subject Tests, but is different for registration online or through the mail. Both processes are laid out in the College Board websites's "SAT Subject Test" section under the "Register" tab.

Dates and Deadlines

SAT Subject Tests share the dates of SAT tests, with the exception of March and July. The dates for each Subject test are different and can all be found on the College Board's website.
