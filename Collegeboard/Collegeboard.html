What is it?

CollegeBoard is a non-profit organization which helps connect students to college opportunities and success. CollegeBoard directly helps students to prepare for the SAT and Advanced Placement Program. College Board offers scholarship applications,research, college planning, as well as college and career search tools. College Board also administers standardized tests, such as the SAT Suite of Assessments, PSAT 8/9, PSAT 10, PSAT/NMSQT, SAT, and SAT subject tests. College Board offers students the opportunity to be entered in to Student Search Services. Student Search Services gives students free information that regards college admissions, financial aid, and scholarships.

College Board gives students access to the Advanced Placement Program which enables high school students to take college-level classes similar in difficulty to first-year college courses. College Board’s also provides a CSS Profile. CSS Profile is an online application for financial aid outside of federal aid. The profile is used by approximately 400 colleges, universities, professional schools, as well as scholarship programs. College Board has many other helpful applications which help students to be successful, but these applications are only available if you make an account.
